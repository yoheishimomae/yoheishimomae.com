About @yoheis

Born in Japan, I moved to beautiful Vancouver BC as a teenager and graduated from Simon Fraser University under School of Technology and Arts, focusing on Media Arts and Interaction Design. I have been working in the tech industry since 2008.

I consider myself a hybrid designer/developer and through work, I have both designed and devloped various mobile applications as well as websites and web applications. Along side with IA, UX and graphic design, I am proficient in Javascript, CSS, HTML, Ruby, Java and PHP.

Currently, I work at Adobe PhoneGap team as a UX Designer.
